ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Magen Chen. Welcome to my personal website.

I am a sixteen-year-old student from Toronto, Canada, who is passionate about music. As a rising senior, I look forward to applying to colleges this year. This summer, I am learning to code at the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program.

Music

At a young age, I began playing the violin and piano. When I was ten years old, I became a violinist in the Canadian Sinfonietta Youth Orchestra (CSYO).

In 2015, I was admitted to an arts-focused high school in Toronto, Cardinal Carter Academy for the Arts, which I currently attend. At school, I am an active member of Arts for the Benefit of Others (ABO), where I performed solo and in an ensemble for senior homes and church services a few times a year. In 2018, I was the second violinist of the pit orchestra in my school's musical production, "Joseph and the Technicolor Dreamcoat."

My hobbies include reading literature, writing, videography, and photography.
